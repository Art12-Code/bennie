High Performance

Bennie Automobile cars are designed to provide high performance, offering superior acceleration, top speed, and handling. They are built using advanced technology such as lightweight materials, powerful engines, and advanced aerodynamics, making them faster and more agile than regular cars.

Cutting-Edge Technology

Bennie Automobile cars are equipped with the latest technology, including high-tech infotainment systems, advanced safety features, and driver assistance technologies. These features enhance the driving experience and provide a level of comfort and convenience that is unmatched by regular cars.

Unique Design

Bennie Automobile cars are designed to be visually stunning, with unique and futuristic designs that set them apart from other cars on the road. They literally caught attention at all costs. These cars are handcrafted and feature premium materials such as leather, carbon fibre, glass fibre, and high-quality metals.

About Us

Bennie Automobile is a Nigerian Automotive Design and Manufacturing company established in 2018. It is a subsidiary of the Bennie Group of Companies headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria with offices and workshops in Jos Plateau and Abuja.

Led by Jerry Isaac Mallo, Bennie Autos was established in 2018 and debuted its first model named Purrie on the 18th of November 2019 with a launch event in Abuja, Nigeria. The Purrie is the first carbon fibre sports car made in Nigeria and was borne out of the Jerry’s passion in auto design and sport car enthusiasm that he nurtured while studying automotive engineering at the prestigious University of Hertfordshire, Hatfield UK. ‘On his return to Nigeria in 2016, he set on the journey to put Nigeria on the map of Luxury Automobile manufacturers. Bennie Automobile is headed towards a future of sustainable and renewable energy in its commitment to clean energy and environmental conservation as it relates to climate change.
